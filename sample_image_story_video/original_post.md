# petty revenge on loud upstairs neighbors

happened a few years back the apartment complex i lived in was being refreshed in phases, and marketed for upscale clients the refreshed apartments came with wood floating floors.
new young family moves in directly above me. guy, gal, and little kid. they're respectful of quiet hours, Til.. 6 months in, gal moves out discreetly, quietly during the day with her boxed belongings with a friend.
my quiet evenings are no longer quiet. someone gave the guy and his kid a big wheel trike. apparently this guy after a long hard day decides it's better to have his kid on a bike with wood floors than being outside.
large plastic wheels on wood floors sound like bowling balls on bowling lanes, except louder.
left a few notes to please keep the noise down and observe quiet hours, no joy. after almost 2 months of little to sleep, i complain to apartment. nope no joy. just blank stares.
3rd month different approach. every guy loves action movies! it's best enjoyed at 11pm at night where folks can appreciate the explosions and gunshots on 7 channel speaker and hi def TV.
amazingly enough the big wheels races, stopped.
